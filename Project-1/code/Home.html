Bio

 Prof. Syed Farrukh Anwar is Vice-Principal and Head of the Department of Civil Engineering. Prof. Farrukh is an illustrious and eminent engineer with capacious technical expertise in Structural Design of  telephone exchange buildings, residential buildings, office buildings, communication towers etc.
